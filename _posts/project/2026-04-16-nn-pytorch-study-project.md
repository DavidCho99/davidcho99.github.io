## Milestones

- Linear regression baseline (closed-form + gradient descent)
- MLP on MNIST (batching + dropout)
- CNN on MNIST (conv + pooling)
- Cross entropy classification (softmax + log-loss)
- Autoencoder (optional: transposed conv decoder)

## Experiment checklist

- Fixed train/val/test split
- Seed control + deterministic settings (as much as practical)
- Track metrics + learning curves
- Keep a simple baseline for comparison
